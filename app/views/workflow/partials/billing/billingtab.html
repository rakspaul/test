% of Gross Revenue

Visto will change a percentage of Gross Revenue (Sum of Billable Amounts for all Line Item run under this Account and its Sub-Accounts).

% of Net Revenue

Visto will charge a percentage of Net Revenue Net Revenue = (Sum of Line Items' Billable Amounts) - ("Retail" Media Cost + Verification + Ad Serving + Data Segment Costs + Misc. Fixed Costs defined for Media Plan)
